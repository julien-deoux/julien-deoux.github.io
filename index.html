    Orbital (2020)

    My first release since 2016's Noise on a black canvas, Orbital is a simple trance song with some orchestral elements spersed in. The intro track Lift Off was inspired by Black Mesa's soundtrack by Joel Nielsen, where some songs where reversed and slowed down to produce ambient textures.

    Hiraeth (2020)

    In early 2020, a friend of mine made an environmental 3D animation as a school project, and asked me to write a soundtrack for it. That video is not available on the Internet, but the music I made is.

    Noise on a black canvas (2016)

    This is an electronic / ambient album I made after falling in love with the Chimera VST instrument, which generates its sound from white noise. White noise quickly became a major part of this record, hence its name.
